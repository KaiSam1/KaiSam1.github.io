Landmarks

Marin County is home to stunning landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge Viewpoint and the historic Point Reyes Lighthouse.

Parks

Enjoy the natural beauty of Muir Woods National Monument, a tranquil haven of ancient redwoods, and the panoramic vistas of Mount Tamalpais State Park.

Museums

Discover the rich cultural history at the Marin History Museum and the Bolinas Museum, offering unique insights into the region's heritage.

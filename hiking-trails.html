Mount Tamalpais State Park

Experience breathtaking views of the Bay Area from Mount Tamalpais. With trails for all skill levels, this park is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.

Muir Woods National Monument

Walk among ancient redwoods in this serene forest. Muir Woods offers shaded trails and unique wildlife, perfect for a peaceful hike.

Point Reyes National Seashore

Explore diverse trails with coastal views, forests, and meadows. Point Reyes is ideal for adventurers seeking a variety of terrains.
